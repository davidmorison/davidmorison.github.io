Order to Disorder in Quasiperiodic Systems

My thesis's centerpiece will be the discovery that quasiperiodic microstructure can lead to bulk transport properties similar to random systems. This is despite quasiperiodic microgeometry that is entirely deterministic.

This research was inspired by the surprising material properties of metallic quasicrystals. Aluminum alloys quasicrystals can be hard, brittle, and poorly conducting, while similar alloys are ductile and conductive.
